About Us

Who we are

We are a restaurant that specialises in Maori kai and are based in the Bay of Islands. The restaurant was started by a couple who are keen on creating a business that would appeal to locals and tourists visiting the area by allowing customers to experience Te Reo and Maori culture.

We hope to be able welcome you to this restaurant and share this experience with you.

Maori kai

'Kai' is the Maori word for 'food'. Traditional kai included kumara, whitebait, cabbage tree, flax seed and taproot. Kaimoana, or 'seafood', also makes up an important part of Maori cusine.
